**Reference app:** Inspired by Droplet / StressWatch water tracking — a high-retention health habit app. **What to build:** A mobile hydration tracking app where users log water intake with one tap, set personalized daily goals (based on weight, climate, activity), and receive smart reminders. Include streak tracking and a weekly intake heatmap. **Key features:** - One-tap water log (custom cup sizes) - Personalized daily goal calculator - Progress ring with percentage - Smart reminder notifications - Weekly/monthly intake history chart - Streak tracker with milestones **Scoring focus:** Logging UX speed, reminder design, and data visualization.
